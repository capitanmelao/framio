# Privacy Policy for Framio

**Last updated: February 1, 2025**

## Information Collection and Use

Framio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.

## Data Storage

- All videos and images are processed locally on your device
- No data is sent to external servers
- Videos and screenshots remain on your device only
- We do not have access to your content

## Permissions

Framio requests the following permissions:
- **Photo Library Access**: To select videos and save screenshots
- **Video Access**: To load and process video files

These permissions are used exclusively for the app's core functionality.

## Third-Party Services

Framio does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Framio does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
